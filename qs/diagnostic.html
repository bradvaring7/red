Diagnostic Questions

Elaborating on the situation:

How many bees are you seeing?

How long have you been seeing these bees?

Where are you seeing them?

Probing for more information:

While I am pulling up your account, would you mind letting me know how your pest activity has been since your last visit?

Are you seeing any particular pests now?

Customer Concerns

Have we been meeting your expectations since you signed up with us?

How has your pest activity been since your last quarterly visit?

Does NOT Count

You may ask the following to the customer if needed, but they will not count as diagnostic questions in your graded call as they are general questions that don’t help better understand the customer's issue.

“Is there anything else I can help you with while on this call?”

“So, you are calling in about ants, correct?”
